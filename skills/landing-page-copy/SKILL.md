# Landing Page Copy Skill

## Voice and Tone

Write copy that is:
- Direct and confident, never desperate or hypey
- Focused on outcomes, not features
- Conversational but professional
- Free of buzzwords, adverbs, and em dashes

## Structure Rules

### Headlines
- Lead with the transformation or outcome
- Keep under 10 words
- No questions. Statements only.

### Subheadlines
- One sentence that clarifies the headline
- Explain the "how" briefly

### Body Copy
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use "you" more than "we"
- One idea per paragraph
- Active voice only

### CTAs
- Action verbs only (Start, Get, Try, Join)
- No "Learn More" or "Click Here"
- Create urgency through value, not pressure

## What to Avoid

- Superlatives (best, amazing, revolutionary)
- Adverbs (extremely, very, really)
- Em dashes
- Exclamation points
- Vague claims without specifics
- Feature dumps without benefits

## Process

1. Identify the single core transformation the product delivers
2. Write the headline around that transformation
3. Support with proof points (numbers, specifics, social proof)
4. End each section with a clear next step

## Example Patterns

**Weak**: "Our amazing app helps you get fit!"
**Strong**: "Track your lifts. See your progress. Get stronger."

**Weak**: "Learn more about our features"
**Strong**: "Start your first workout"
